## Integrity Principles

- Keep the original student authorship clear.
- Cite data sources and third-party material.
- Record material changes to data, notebooks, dashboards, and documentation.
- Do not hide generated, copied, or externally assisted work if disclosure is required.
- Preserve the academic integrity declaration in `admin/academic_integrity_declaration.docx`.

## Acceptable Maintenance

Maintenance changes may include:

- correcting spelling, formatting, and path references;
- reorganizing files into clearer folders;
- adding validation scripts and tests;
- documenting assumptions and limitations;
- correcting verified data-cleaning mistakes with evidence from raw files.

## Change Evidence

Where a processed data value is changed, document:

- the affected file;
- the affected record;
- the previous value;
- the corrected value;
- the source used to confirm the correction.
