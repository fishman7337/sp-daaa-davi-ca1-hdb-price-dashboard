## Purpose

The project explores HDB resale flat transactions in Singapore and presents visual insights through Tableau. The cleaned datasets support analysis of resale price variation by location, flat type, floor area, remaining lease, and proximity features.

## Main Questions

- How do resale prices vary across towns and flat types?
- How does floor area relate to resale price and price per square foot?
- How do remaining lease and transaction period affect resale patterns?
- What location features may be associated with resale price differences?
